# KaiProva

**The verified low-carbon beef platform. Every footprint followed.**

KaiProva tracks beef animals from birth to box with per-animal carbon footprinting validated against peer-reviewed AgResearch Life Cycle Assessment science. The platform connects dairy beef farmers, meat processors, dairy companies, and corporate buyers through verified carbon data and supply orchestration.

### What's here

| Repository | Description |
|------------|-------------|

| [**lca-methodology**](https://github.com/kaiprova/lca-methodology) | Carbon verification methodology — AgResearch LCA science, validation approach, reporting standards |

### The science

- 5 peer-reviewed AgResearch papers
- 54 validated LCA scenarios
- Up to 48% lower carbon footprint than the NZ beef average
- The lowest verified carbon footprint of any beef production system published globally
